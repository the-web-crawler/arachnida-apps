Privacy Policy for Color Dash

Effective Date: April 22, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Arachnida ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with your use of the Color Dash mobile application (the "App"). Your privacy is important to us.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information You Provide: We currently do not require you to provide any personal information directly (such as name, email, or account details) to use the core gameplay features of the App.

  • Information Collected Automatically (Third-Party Services - AdMob): The App uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google, to display advertisements. To serve relevant ads, AdMob automatically collects certain information when you use the App. This information may include, but is not limited to:

    • Device identifiers (such as the Advertising ID)
    • IP address
    • Device type, operating system information, and device settings
    • Approximate geolocation data (typically derived from your IP address)
    • App usage data
    • Information about your interactions with advertisements shown in the App (e.g., ad views, clicks)

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality.
  • To display third-party advertisements through Google AdMob, which helps support the App's development and allows us to offer it for free. AdMob uses the collected information to personalize or non-personalize ads shown to you, measure ad performance, limit ad frequency, and combat fraud and abuse.

4. Sharing of Information

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not share personal information collected directly by us (currently none) with third parties, except as necessary to comply with the law, enforce our terms, or protect our rights or the safety of others.
  • Information collected automatically by Google AdMob is processed by Google according to their own privacy policies. We do not have direct control over the data collected solely by AdMob for advertising purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

This App uses Google AdMob. Google's use of advertising identifiers is subject to their policies.

  • You can learn more about how Google collects and uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
  • Google's general Privacy Policy can be found here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Advertising Opt-Out

You can limit ad tracking or reset your device’s advertising identifier by adjusting your Android operating system settings. For up‑to‑date instructions, please see:

  • Manage your ad privacy settings on Android - Google Help

7. Data Security

Because we do not collect personal information directly, we do not store user data on our servers. Google AdMob processes any data it collects under its own security and privacy policies. See Google’s Privacy Policy for details.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 in the United States (as defined by the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) or under the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below these ages. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately using the contact information below, and we will take steps to delete the information as soon as possible.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Effective Date" at the top to reflect material changes and, where required by law, provide notice within the App or by email informing you of any significant revisions. Non-material changes may be posted without additional notice. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
